About the Society

Advancing the actuarial profession in Greece and supporting our members through lifelong learning, professional standards, and global representation.

Our Mission & Vision

The Hellenic Actuarial Society (HAS) is the sole representative body of the actuarial profession in Greece. Since our establishment in 1979, we have been committed to promoting the science and practice of actuaries.

Our vision is to maintain the highest professional and ethical standards, ensuring that actuaries remain at the forefront of financial risk management, insurance, and social security systems.

We are proud full members of the International Actuarial Association (IAA) and the Actuarial Association of Europe (AAE), ensuring our members hold internationally recognized credentials.

A Legacy of Excellence

1979

Foundation of the Society in Athens

1999

Full membership into step-IAA & AAE

2015

Introduction of the CERA credential

Organization & Leadership

The Society is governed by a Board of Directors, elected every two years by the General Assembly of Full Members. The Board works diligently alongside specialized committees to advance our educational, regulatory, and public relations goals.
